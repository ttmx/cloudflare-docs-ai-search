---
title: CNI maintenance alerts
description: Subscribe to email alerts for planned maintenance on CNI v1 circuits
date: 2025-06-20
---

Customers using Cloudflare Network Interconnect with the v1 dataplane can now subscribe to maintenance alert emails. These alerts notify you of planned maintenance windows that may affect your CNI circuits.

For more information, refer to [Monitoring and alerts](/network-interconnect/monitoring-and-alerts/).
