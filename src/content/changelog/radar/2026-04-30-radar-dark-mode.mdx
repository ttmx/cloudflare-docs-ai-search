[**Radar**](/radar/) now supports **dark mode**. A theme selector in the upper right corner of the page lets users explicitly choose between three display options:

- **Light** — standard light theme
- **Dark** — full dark theme
- **System** — follows the operating system preference

![Screenshot of the theme selector showing Light, Dark, and System options](~/assets/images/radar/dark-mode-theme-selector.png)

The selected theme applies consistently across all Radar pages and widgets.

![Screenshot of the Cloudflare Radar overview page in dark mode](~/assets/images/radar/dark-mode-overview.png)

The theme choice also applies to shared and embedded graphs.

Try it out at [Cloudflare Radar](https://radar.cloudflare.com).
