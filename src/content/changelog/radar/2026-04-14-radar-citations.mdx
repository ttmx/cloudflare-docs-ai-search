[**Radar**](/radar/) shareable widgets now include a **generate citation** action, making it easier to reference [Cloudflare Radar](https://radar.cloudflare.com) data in research papers and other publications.

![Screenshot of the generate citation icon in the widget action bar](~/assets/images/radar/citation-action-icon.png)

Select the citation icon to open a modal with five supported citation styles:

- **BibTeX**
- **APA**
- **MLA**
- **Chicago**
- **RIS**

![Screenshot of the citation modal with format options](~/assets/images/radar/citation-modal.png)

Explore the feature on any shareable widget at [Cloudflare Radar](https://radar.cloudflare.com).
