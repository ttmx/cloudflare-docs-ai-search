---
title: Internet outage notifications for devices
description: Internet outage and traffic anomaly notifications for all Cloudflare One Client devices
date: 2026-04-28
---

[Digital Experience](/cloudflare-one/insights/dex/) will display a dashboard notification when an Internet outage or traffic anomaly may impact a [Cloudflare One Client](/cloudflare-one/team-and-resources/devices/cloudflare-one-client/) device based on its geographic location or network connection.

This Internet outage and traffic anomaly data is pulled from [Cloudflare Radar](https://radar.cloudflare.com/). All Internet outage and traffic anomaly observations can be viewed in the [Radar Outage Center](https://radar.cloudflare.com/outage-center).
