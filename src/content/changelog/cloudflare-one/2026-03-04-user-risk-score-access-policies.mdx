You can now use [user risk scores](/cloudflare-one/team-and-resources/users/risk-score/) in your [Access policies](/cloudflare-one/access-controls/policies/). The new **User Risk Score** selector allows you to create Access policies that respond to user behavior patterns detected by Cloudflare's risk scoring system, including impossible travel, high DLP policy matches, and more.

For more information, refer to [Use risk scores in Access policies](/cloudflare-one/team-and-resources/users/risk-score/#use-risk-scores-in-access-policies).
