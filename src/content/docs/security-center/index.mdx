## Availability

Cloudflare Security Center is available to customers on all plans.

The frequency of automatic security scans depends on your Cloudflare plan, ranging from every 7 days on Free, Pro, and Business plans to every 3 days on Enterprise plans. Refer to [Scan frequency](/security/security-insights/how-it-works/#scan-frequency) for more information.

If you have any comments, questions, or bugs to report, create a post in the [Cloudflare Community forum](https://community.cloudflare.com/c/security/security-center/65).

## Limitations

- Users with an [Administrator Read Only](/fundamentals/manage-members/roles/#account-scoped-roles) role cannot access the Cloudflare Security Center.
- Only Cloudflare accounts with at least one Business or Enterprise zone (domain on your account), or accounts on the Teams Standard or Teams Enterprise plans, can manually start a new scan.
