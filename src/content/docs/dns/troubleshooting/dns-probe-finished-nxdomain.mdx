## Background

`DNS_PROBE_FINISHED_NXDOMAIN` indicates that the DNS lookup completed and the result was that the domain does not exist. `DNS_PROBE_FINISHED` means that the DNS probe ran to completion and `NXDOMAIN` stands for non-existent domain. Together, these messages mean that the DNS resolver determined the requested domain has no associated <GlossaryTooltip term="DNS record" link="/dns/manage-dns-records/">DNS records</GlossaryTooltip>.

Though visitors sometimes encounter this error — or similarly worded messages from Safari, Edge, or Firefox — because of network or local DNS issues, it might point to an issue with your DNS records in Cloudflare.
