## Troubleshooting

If you received a tunnel health alert but are unsure whether it affects your traffic, refer to [Troubleshoot connectivity](/cloudflare-wan/troubleshooting/connectivity/) to determine whether the alert is relevant.

If your tunnels show as unhealthy or degraded, refer to [Troubleshoot tunnel health](/cloudflare-wan/troubleshooting/tunnel-health/) for common issues and solutions.
