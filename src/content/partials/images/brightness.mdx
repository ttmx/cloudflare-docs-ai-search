### `brightness`

Adjusts the image's overall luminance using a multiplier.

- `1` (default) — No change to the original brightness.
- `< 1.0` — Darkens the image, e.g. `0.5` is half as bright.
- `> 1.0` — Lightens the image, e.g. `2` is twice as bright.
