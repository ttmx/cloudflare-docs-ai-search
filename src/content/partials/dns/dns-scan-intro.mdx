Cloudflare can [automatically scan for your records](/dns/zone-setups/reference/dns-quick-scan/) and add them to the <GlossaryTooltip term="zone" link="/dns/concepts/#zone">DNS zone</GlossaryTooltip> for you, or you can add records manually. These records show up under your domain on the [**DNS Records**](https://dash.cloudflare.com/?to=/:account/:zone/dns/records) page of the dashboard.
