Identity-based selectors include:

* **SAML Attributes**
* **User Email**
* **User Group Emails**
* **User Group IDs**
* **User Group Names**
* **User Name**

To use identity-based selectors, enable **Traffic and DNS mode** in the Cloudflare One Client and enroll your user in your organization. For more information, refer to [Identity-based policies](/cloudflare-one/traffic-policies/identity-selectors/).
